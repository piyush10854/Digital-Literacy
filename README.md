# Project Overview

This Digital Ambassador Project focuses on building digital literacy, professional communication skills, online safety awareness, and platform engagement. The project consists of five modules designed to improve students' understanding of responsible digital behaviour, professional communication, and cybersecurity awareness.

Each module includes practical tasks, documentation, and reflections to demonstrate learning outcomes and real-world application.

---

# Module Summaries

## Module 1 — Digital Awareness Presentation

**Folder: `task-1-presentation/`

**Description:**
This module focuses on creating a digital awareness presentation to educate peers about responsible technology usage, online safety, and digital citizenship.

---

## Module 2 — Digital Portfolio Setup

**Folder:** `task-2-portfolio/`

**Description:**
This module involved creating professional online profiles and building a digital portfolio. The objective was to establish an online presence and showcase skills, projects, and achievements.

**Platforms Created:**

* GitHub Profile
* LinkedIn Profile
* Portfolio Website

---

## Module 3 — Platform Engagement & Activity

**Folder:** `task-3-platforms/`

**Description:**
This module focused on exploring digital platforms for learning and collaboration. Activities included participating in discussions, engaging with communities, and documenting learning outcomes.

---

## Module 4 — Professional Email & Etiquette

**Folder:** `task-4-email-etiquette/`

**Description:**
This module focused on professional communication skills. It includes formal email drafts, social media etiquette guidelines, and a case scenario demonstrating the importance of responsible digital communication.

---

## Module 5 — Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention

**Folder:** `task-5-cybercrime/`

**Description:**
This module focuses on cybercrime awareness, specifically UPI fraud and online scams. It includes a real-world case study, prevention tips, and reporting information.
