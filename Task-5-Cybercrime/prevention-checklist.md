#Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention Guide
#Stay Safe Online Checklist (For College Students in India)

#Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

1. Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.
2. Always verify payment requests before approving any UPI collect request. If you did not initiate the payment, decline it immediately.
3. Enable screen lock, fingerprint, or face lock on your phone to prevent unauthorized access.
4. Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, email, or social media.
5. Use only official apps downloaded from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
6. Do not use public Wi‑Fi for banking, online payments, or accessing sensitive information.
7. Regularly update your phone, apps, and antivirus software to stay protected from security vulnerabilities.
8. Check website URLs carefully before entering login or payment details. Look for HTTPS and trusted domains.
9. Immediately block your SIM and bank accounts if your phone is lost or stolen.
10. Monitor bank statements and UPI transaction history regularly for suspicious activity.

#Financial / UPI Safety (Important)

 @ Never approve a "Collect Request" unless you are making a payment.
 @ Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN. If asked, it's a scam.
