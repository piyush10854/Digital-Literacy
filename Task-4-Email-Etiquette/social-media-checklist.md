#Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

#Social Media Do's

 Think before posting anything online
 Use professional language and respectful tone
 Verify information before sharing
 Maintain privacy and protect personal information
 Use social media for learning and networking
 Give proper credit when sharing content

#Social Media Don'ts

 Don't share offensive or inappropriate content
 Don't post confidential academic or personal information
 Don't engage in online arguments or harassment
 Don't spread misinformation or fake news
 Don't overshare personal details
 Don't post content that harms your professional image
